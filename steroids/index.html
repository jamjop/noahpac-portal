Corticosteroid Equivalency Reference (approximate)

Clinical caution required. Equivalency conversions are approximations based on relative glucocorticoid potency and do not account for individual pharmacokinetic variability, hepatic function, or drug interactions. Mineralocorticoid activity differs substantially between agents (highest in hydrocortisone/cortisone, minimal to none in methylprednisolone/triamcinolone/dexamethasone/betamethasone) and may affect fluid/electrolyte status independent of glucocorticoid dosing. Tapering guidance reflects general principles only — individualize based on underlying condition, response, and risk of adrenal insufficiency.

Equivalency ratios and HPA suppression thresholds from standard endocrinology references (Endocrine Society, UpToDate) and clinical pharmacology texts. Not medical advice.
