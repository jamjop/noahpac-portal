Wound Type
Quality, contamination, and age of wound

Tetanus Vaccination History
Prior immunization status and time since last booster
Vaccine products
  • Tdap (Adacel, Boostrix) — preferred if never received
  • Td — if Tdap previously given or contraindicated
  • TIG (HyperTET) — 250 units IM, separate site from vaccine
  • If both needed: administer at separate sites simultaneously
Tetanus-prone features
  • Contaminated (soil, saliva, feces)
  • Puncture wounds, crush injuries, avulsions
  • Animal or human bites
  • Burns or frostbite
  • Wounds >6 hours old
  • Devitalized or ischemic tissue
Recommendation
Based on ACIP tetanus prophylaxis recommendations. Tdap/Td: 0.5 mL IM deltoid. TIG: 250 units IM contralateral site. Not medical advice.
