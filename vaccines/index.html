Summarizes ACIP, AAP, and ACOG immunization recommendations; verify with current schedules and individualize based on prior vaccination history, contraindications, and clinical judgment. Data reflects 2026 ACIP schedule including ACIP 2025 updates (clesrovimab/Enwina, Penbraya MenABCWY, JYNNEOS mpox, influenza 2025–26). AAP differences: HPV from age 9, LAIV preferred ages 2–8, MenB routine for all adolescents 16–23. ACOG differences: RSV maternal vaccine (Abrysvo at 32–36 weeks), Tdap optimal timing 27–32 weeks, HPV deferred during pregnancy, IIV strongly recommended over LAIV in pregnancy.
