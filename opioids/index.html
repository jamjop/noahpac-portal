Equianalgesic Reference (approximate)
Clinical caution required. Equianalgesic conversions are approximations based on population averages. Individual responses vary significantly. Always apply clinical judgment, reassess frequently, and titrate to effect. When rotating opioids, use the lowest reasonable conversion ratio and adjust for incomplete cross-tolerance. Consult pain management or palliative care for complex patients.
